VISION

A diocesan catholic school community that is founded on the oneness of Heart of Jesus and Mary and passionate in the
communal work of integral human development of young towards the fullness of life.

MISSION

Inspired by and devoted to the oneness of Heart of Jesus and Mary, OLSCHO is committed to:
1. Develop among students modern-world skills for them to succeed as empowered global citizens; and
2. Reinforce values formation to produce professionals who are responsive to the changing needs of industry and society; and
3. Strengthen linkages and industry partners for continuous development.
